Coffee Recipe

Coffee is one of the most popular drinks enjoyed worldwide for its rich taste and energy-boosting effect. A simple homemade coffee can be prepared quickly with just a few ingredients. It is a perfect beverage for mornings or whenever you need refreshment.

Ingredients

  • Milk – 1 cup
  • Instant coffee – 1 tsp
  • Sugar – 1–2 tsp (as per taste)

Steps

  1. Boil milk in a pan or heat it in a cup.
  2. Add instant coffee powder and sugar.
  3. Stir well until everything dissolves.
  4. Pour into a cup and enjoy hot.

A simple sandwich is one of the easiest and quickest snacks to make. It requires very few ingredients and is perfect for breakfast, evening tea, or a light meal. Fresh bread with a little butter and basic vegetables like cucumber and tomato makes it healthy, tasty, and filling. Since it needs no cooking, anyone can prepare it in just a couple of minutes.

Ingredients

  • Bread slices – 2
  • Butter – 1 tsp
  • Cucumber slices – 4–5
  • Tomato slices – 3–4
  • Salt & pepper – a pinch

Steps

  1. Spread butter on both bread slices.
  2. Place cucumber and tomato slices on one bread.
  3. Sprinkle a little salt and pepper.
  4. Cover with the other bread slice.
  5. Cut and serve fresh.
